Welcome

Welcome to Breathe Yoga, where movement meets mindfulness. We offer a calm, supportive space for all levels—whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a long-standing practice.

Our classes blend breath awareness, strength building, and gentle flow to help you reconnect with your body and mind. Every session is guided by certified instructors who are passionate about creating inclusive, intentional experiences.

Whether you're looking for stress relief, physical balance, or a warm and welcoming community, we're here to support your journey.

Join us for in-person or virtual classes throughout the week.

Community Yoga Retreat

Take a break from the noise and reconnect with your inner calm at our weekend Community Yoga Retreat. We believe in yoga as a lifelong practice—not a performance.

Our studio is a place to reset, recharge, and return to yourself without judgment or pressure. Located just outside the city, this peaceful gathering includes guided yoga, meditation, nourishing meals, and time in nature.

Your practice starts exactly where you are—and so do we. Open to all levels, the retreat is a chance to unwind, reflect, and build deeper community through shared practice.
